Step 1- Wallet Setup

Welcome aboard! Start by getting MetaMask on your desktop browser or a Wallet Connect-compatible wallet like Trust Wallet for your mobile. Desktop users, MetaMask is ideal for a smooth purchase process. Mobile users, Trust Wallet or MetaMask connected through Wallet Connect is your go-to.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f3vwOxSLBUc

Step 2 - Purchase Process

Ready to hold? Simply click on pay with metamask, input the amount you wish to obtain, and hit 'Confirm Payment'. A prompt will pop up from your metamask wallet for transaction confirmation, where you’ll also see the gas fees. Note: Purchasing with USDT/BNB may involve two approvals—one to okay the contract and another for the actual payment.

Step 3 - Token Airdrop

After our presale ends, purchased TaxiCoin tokens will be airdropped directly into the wallet that was connected to our website at the time of purchase. Your dashboard, accessible by connecting your wallet to our site, will show you all your token details and values.
